# aum. Positioning

## Identity
**aum.** — artisanal botanical soap brand. Family project. Production in Subachoque, Cundinamarca, Colombia. Small batches, handmade.

## Territory
Bienestar botánico consciente. Intersection of: botánica, ritual, naturaleza, presencia. NOT a cosmetics brand. NOT an esoteric/spiritual brand.

## Four Pillars
1. **Botánica** — plants are the core, formulas inspired by traditional botanical use
2. **Ritual** — personal care as a moment of pause and presence
3. **Naturaleza** — from the earth, back to it. Every ingredient has a real name and origin
4. **Presencia** — the capacity to stop and reconnect with yourself

## Philosophy
Plants have cared for skin for thousands of years. aum. adapts that tradition to contemporary life. Each product creates small moments of reconnection within the daily routine. Crystals are symbolic — a physical reminder of intention. Never therapeutic claims.

## Differentiators (uncontested territory in Colombia)
- Editorial aesthetic in an all-lo-fi market
- Full ingredient transparency (INCI + common names, always visible)
- Real family origin story (Subachoque, verifiable)
- Collection concept (7 energy centers, Crown→Root)
- Crystal as lasting artifact (felt, not decorative)
- Saponification process: preserves nutritive oils + natural glycerin
- No palm oil (sustainability + rapid rancidity avoidance)

## What aum. Is NOT (in customer-facing copy)
- A cosmetics brand · A spiritual/esoteric brand · A "startup" or "emprendimiento" · A mass-market brand with artisanal marketing

## Competitive Context
Colombian artisanal soap market = 100% lo-fi. No editorial presence exists. aum. occupies uncontested territory. Do NOT claim as primary: "Natural / artesanal / hecho a mano / sin químicos" — only use when backed by specific ingredient or process.

## Confirmed Brand Decisions
- Crystal role = symbolic only — never therapeutic claims
- Energy centers = bienestar contemporáneo lens, not religious doctrine
- Ingredient transparency = innegociable
- The period in "aum." = the only graphic element of the brand
- No palm oil in any formula — emphasize subtly
- Saponification + natural glycerin = key differentiator story
- Communication = sencilla, entendible, cercana, respetuosa
