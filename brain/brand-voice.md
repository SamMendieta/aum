# aum. Brand Voice

## The Soul — Andrea's 8 Principles

| # | Principle | Meaning |
|---|-----------|---------|
| 1 | Belleza en cada frase | Every sentence beautiful when read aloud. Ugly connectors kill the spell. |
| 2 | Cercanía no distancia | Speak to YOU, not "whoever receives it." Warm = close, specific, personal. |
| 3 | Sencillez | Simple words, short sentences. If she pauses to parse it, copy failed. |
| 4 | Respeto | Never imply competitors are fake or readers need educating. |
| 5 | El cristal vive | Crystal is felt, not explained. Not decorative, not esoteric — energetic. |
| 6 | La piel habla | Describe how skin FEELS after. The result IS the product. |
| 7 | El proceso es la historia | Saponification preserves oils + generates natural glycerin. Tell as quality story, not attack. |
| 8 | Sutileza | Differentiate gently. Quality speaks in the product itself, not in what it "isn't." |

## Voice Reference — The Founders' Own Words

> "Déjate envolver por la armonía de la albahaca, geranio, verbena limón y atlas de cedro. Este jabón es un abrazo aromático que eleva tu espíritu y nutre tu ser."

> "Sumérgete en la fragancia de romero, atlas de cedro, naranja y benjuí para atraer vibraciones positivas."

> "Déjate seducir por la deliciosa mezcla de vainilla cremosa y sándalo aterciopelado, mezcla cautivadora que te invita a explorar tu mundo interno."

These are STYLE REFERENCES. Study for voice, register, emotional temperature. Patterns: sensory verbs of immersion open. Oils named with texture adjectives. Result clauses gently energetic/spiritual.

## Tone Rules

- Headlines: sentence case, max 8 words, never bold
- Body: short sentences, breathing rhythm, no filler, no em dashes
- Crystal: always "cristal de" — never "piedra de"
- Energy: always "centro de energía" — never "chakra" (in web copy)
- Brand name: always `aum.` — lowercase, with period. Never AUM, Aum, aum
- Español: cálido, limpio, sin relleno corporativo
- Approved sensory verbs: disfruta, déjate envolver, déjate seducir, sumérgete, experimenta
- The founders' spiritual register ("vibraciones positivas", "nutre tu ser") is genuine voice — do not flatten

## Closeness Test (Andrea's Litmus)

"Would Andrea say this to a customer she's met in person, warmly, over the counter?" If no → rewrite.
