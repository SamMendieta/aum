# aum. Audience

## Primary: La Exploradora Consciente
Colombian woman, 25-38 (brand intent: 30-65; copy focus: 30-45). Urban: Bogotá (launch priority), Medellín, Cali, Bucaramanga, Barranquilla. Also: foreign women living in Colombia. Active — yoga, pilates, running, strength training. Instagram-native, visually literate, skeptical of greenwashing.

## Discovery Path
Instagram → profile visit → website (credibility check) → WhatsApp (purchase).
Mobile-first. Evaluating in seconds on phone screen after seeing brand content on IG. Not browsing — confirming trust.

## Job to Be Done
Confirm aum. is real — not another lo-fi "emprendimiento de jabones" — before committing to WhatsApp. Understand what makes it different. Find purchase path without friction.

## Emotional Goal
Calm recognition, not excitement. "A brand made exactly for me, before everyone else finds it." The interface should feel like the product: unhurried, specific, present.

## Secondary: Gift Buyer
Wants something original and conceptually complete, not a candle. Desktop browsing, exploratory.

## Language That Resonates
Specific ingredients by name, sensory descriptions, real place names (Subachoque), process transparency, ritual framing (intentional, not routine), gentle spirituality, "tú" address.

## Language That Repels
Wellness buzzwords without substance, moral instruction, greenwashing signals ("natural" without proof), clinical/academic vocabulary, aggressive selling, impersonal distance, demographic flattery ("diseñado para la mujer que se cuida").
